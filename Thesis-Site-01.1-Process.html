USSR Housing Renewal Process

Process

This is the process of change for the following buildings. We begin with removing the red, which is various cuts on the building, and the roof. The roof will end up being reused in the following slide. The roof will now act as a mezzanine, architectural piece, and a view of sunrise and sunset. We go on to add structure and the core, as well as the main circulation. The structure is added, using beams as the floor plate structure, hung by thick wires off the truss as the main point of structure overtop, giving a 'floating' effect. Floor plates and glazing is finally added.
