The Site

The site researched was KIM Street, Volgograd, 400001. Specifically, buildings 10A, 12, 12A, and 14A. These are highlighted below in the red box. Slide to view the after and before between the changes. The pink are the additions made to the existing buildings. This site houses over 200 units, and makes for a perfect candidate for rebuilding. These additions can be applied to one or more buildings, whether it is in a complex or singular throughout the country.

These four building create three different approaches to this case study. One, a cantilever addition, two, a bridge addition, and three, a connecting addition. These will not only give about 1.5 times the space per unit (by law), but also include amenities, views, and structural additions.
