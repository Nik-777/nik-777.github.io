Revitalizing Soviet Architecture

Project Overview

Explore the transformation of Soviet-era housing into modern, sustainable living spaces. This project looks into the historical significance, design innovations, and future prospects of housing renewal. This is a section model of a building, one side showing the facade, while the other showing the inside floor plates and occupiable spaces. Seen is also the circulation, and activated areas like the sitting roof stairs and sloped rooftop with theatres and views.
